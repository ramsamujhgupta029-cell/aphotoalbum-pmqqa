Photo Album

​A photo album is just a way to keep your photos safe and show them off, whether it's a real book or something on your phone or computer.

📚 Types of Photo Albums

1. Real-Life Albums

*   Slip-in Albums: You slide photos into plastic pockets. Simple, not much room to change things up.
*   Self-stick Albums: Photos stick to pages with glue under a clear sheet. Ok for different sizes, but the glue can turn yellow as time passes..
*   Scrapbook Albums: Blank pages let you add photos, notes, and cool stuff. You can do whatever you want with these.
*   Classic Corner Albums: Use corners or glue to stick pics. Old-school way to keep them safe.
*   Ring Binder Albums: Add or take out pages easy. Good for changing things around.

2. Digital Albums

*   Computer Albums: Use software to make albums on your computer, like PDFs.
*   Cloud Albums: Store pics online to share and back them up.
*   Phone App Albums: Your phone makes albums for you, usually by date or place.
*   Digital Frames: Show pics on a screen that flips through them.

🧱 Parts of a Real Album

*   Cover: Keeps the album safe, could be leather, cloth, or plastic.
*   Binding: Holds the pages in, could be glued, sewn, or with rings.
*   Pages: Made of cardboard, thick paper, or plastic.
*   Clear Sheets: Keep dust and crud off your photos.

🧾 Sizes

*   Normal photo sizes:
    *   4×6 inch (the usual)
    *   5×7 inch
    *   8×10 inch
*   How many photos albums hold:
    *   Small (20–50 photos)
    *   Medium (100–200 photos)
    *   Large (300+ photos)

🧴 What They're Made Of

*   Paper or Cardstock: For light albums.
*   Plastic: For those clear sleeves.
*   Cloth or Leather: For fancy covers.
*   Acid-free stuff: Keeps photos from turning yellow.

🕰️ How to Keep Them Safe

*   Keep albums cool, dry, and dark.
*   Keep them away from sun and wetness.
*   Use acid-free paper to not hurt the photos.
*   Hold photos by the edges, or wear gloves.
*   Scan old photos to have a backup.

🎨 Make It Your Own

*   Add labels and dates.
*   Use stickers and tape to decorate.
*   Make themes, like trips or weddings.
*   Add stuff like tickets and notes.

💾 What Digital Albums Can Do

*   Sort by date, face, or where they were taken.
*   Back up and share to the cloud.
*   Make slideshows easy.
*   Fix photos with brightness, cropping, and filters.
*   Tag and name pics to find them later.

🔍 What Albums Are Used For

*   Family memories
*   Trip diaries
*   Weddings
*   Baby pics
*   Remembering people
*   Work portfolios
*   Art stuff

📦 Other Ways to Keep Photos

*   Boxes or sleeves
*   Scrapbooks
*   External hard drives or cloud
*   Printed photo books
